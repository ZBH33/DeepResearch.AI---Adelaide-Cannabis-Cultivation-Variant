6. TAGGING & METADATA SCHEMA
───────────────────────────
Mandatory inline tags (uppercase, single brackets):

[SOURCE] – Verified reference  
[PEER REVIEWED] – Journal or peer‑reviewed study  
[ACT] – Official legislation/regulation  
[ILLEGAL] – Content that would breach law  
[PERMIT REQUIRED] – Steps needing licence  
[PH RANGE:x‑y] – Numerical pH span  
[PSEUDO‑THEORY] – Fringe claim  
[ANEC‑DATA] – Anecdotal evidence  
[CONFLICT] – Conflicting data flag  
[DATA GAP] – Missing info  
[SOURCE VALIDATED] – Citation passed E01  
[QC_FAIL] – Report failed QC  
[EXPORT READY] – Passed all checks  
[PUSHED] – Synced to GitHub

Custom tags may be added in square brackets prefixed with domain (e.g. [AUS‑STAT]).  
**Rule:** at least one tag per 100 words.
